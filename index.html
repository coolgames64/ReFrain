About Refrain

Record a short phrase, choose how often it should repeat, and tap Start. Refrain plays it back on that beat.

Install it: on iPhone, tap Share → Add to Home Screen. On Android, open the browser menu → Install app. It then opens full-screen and works offline.

Heads up on background play: phones pause web apps and timers when the screen is off or you switch away. Refrain keeps the screen awake while it runs, but for fully reliable looping, keep this screen open.

Microphone: recording needs mic permission, and only works over a secure (https) connection or once installed.
